Shipibo-Conibo


The Shipibo-Conibo are an indigenous people along the Ucayali River in the Amazon rainforest in Peru. They have lived in the Amazonian rainforest for millennia. Many of their traditions are still practiced, such as ayahuasca medicine work. Their medicine songs have inspired artistic tradition and decorative designs found in their clothing, pottery, tools, and textiles.
